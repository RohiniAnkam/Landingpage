Masala products are blends of ground spices used in Indian cooking to add flavor, aroma, and color to a wide range of dishes. They can be a dry powder mix, a paste, or whole spices, and their ingredients vary by region and intended use, with common components including cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili, and garam masala spices like cinnamon and cloves.
